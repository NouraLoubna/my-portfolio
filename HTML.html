My Thesis Project

My final year project (PFE) focuses on the analysis of error sources and residual signals in InSAR time series over the Oran region. The work involves:

  • SBAS technique implementation with MintPy
  • Phase unwrapping
  • Tropospheric error corrections
  • Ionospheric error corrections (IRI-2016 model, GIMs, Split-Spectrum)
  • Orbital errors and bias management
  • Projection of displacement and tectonic/seasonal analysis

This research contributes to improving the accuracy of geodetic measurements and helps understand geophysical processes in the Oran region.
